Recommended for colour printing: a 3MF with each layer as a separately-coloured object, so Bambu Studio keeps the colours and you can assign a filament per layer. Loads flat, buildings up, pre-scaled to 200 mm.

STL/OBJ are single-colour (the format can't store colours — that's why a plain STL loads as one colour). STL loads flat, pre-scaled to 200 mm. GLB (glTF) opens in Blender & most 3D apps in real-world metres. The grey base sheet is preview-only and is not included in these 3D files.

A flat greyscale map on an A3 sheet (297 × 420 mm, north up), sized so the printed 200 mm model sits in the lower two-thirds at 1:1. Print at 100% scale on A3.

The raised suburb/postcode title as a separate 3D-printable 3MF, pre-scaled to match the 200 mm model. Its height equals the map's highest elevation.

Cache

Elevation tiles and OpenStreetMap lookups are cached in your browser for 30 days, so revisiting an area doesn't re-download it. Clear it to free space or force fresh data.
