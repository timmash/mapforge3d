GLB (glTF) opens in Blender & most 3D apps, in real-world metres · STL is pre-scaled to 200 mm for 3D printing · OBJ is universally supported (Rhino, SketchUp Pro, Maya…). The grey base sheet is preview-only and is not included in these 3D files.

A flat greyscale map on an A3 sheet (297 × 420 mm, north up), sized so the printed 200 mm model sits in the lower two-thirds at 1:1. Print at 100% scale on A3.
